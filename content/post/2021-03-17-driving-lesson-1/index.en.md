- Right-A-Way/Protected Turn <= when there is a right turn traffic signal
- Unprotected Turn <= when there is no right turn traffic signal
- you need to yield to:
  1. the traffic coming from the opposite direction
  2. the traffic coming from the opposite direction that’s gonna turn right
  3. pedestrian on your left side
- hand gestures
  - extend the arm out along with the shoulder - turn left
  - 90 degree angle, pointing upward - right turn
  - 90 degree pointing downward - stop/slowdown
- check all those (point to those, must be right, otherwise fail)
  - headlights
  - windshield wiper
  - emergency parking brake
  - emergency flasher
  - defroster
  - horn
- adjust steering wheel
  - seat position
  - rear-view mirrors
  - side-view mirror
- lane switching (smog: signals, mirrors, look over, go)
  - signal, (rear-view) mirror, (side-view) mirror, shoulder
  - **don’t slow down!!!**
  
- pass through crossroad - traffic checking - looking left and right

#### Stop sign
- completely stop, count 1, 2, 3; and then move ahead and check the incoming traffic (exaggerate movement)
- all way
- two way

#### red flashing traffic light/ beacon => it’s a stop sign

#### if two cars arrive at the same time, what to do?
- The right side car goes first

#### left lane is the fast lane, stay on the right

#### right turn, left turn
- don’t press brake pedal when turning, hit gas pedal slightly when turning and hit harder after the turn is finished

#### when encounter a turn that asks to slow down,
- slow down when turn and press the gas pedal to speed up right after the turn

#### left turn
- catch up with the cars in the front without a big cap
